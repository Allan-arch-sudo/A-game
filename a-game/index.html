Training Science
Principles That Work

Master these and you'll out-train most gym-goers

📈

Progressive Overload

Add 1–2 reps every week or slow your tempo. Muscles only grow when challenged beyond what they're used to.

😴

Sleep = Real Gains

Muscle is built during sleep, not the workout. 7–9 hours. Skimping kills gains faster than skipping sessions.

🥩

Protein First

Target 1.6–2g per kg of bodyweight daily. Build every meal around a quality protein source.

🧠

Mind-Muscle Focus

Think about the muscle you're training. Studies show up to 35% more activation vs mindless movement.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Even 2% dehydration cuts strength output significantly. Drink before, during, and after every session.

📅

Consistency Wins

3 sessions per week every week beats 7-day bursts followed by burnout. Show up — relentlessly.
